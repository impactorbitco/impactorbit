### Background

Orbital Innovations, a SpaceTech startup, needed a sustainable marketing campaign to highlight their eco-conscious satellite deployment methods.

### Objectives

* Increase brand awareness in the European space sector.
* Communicate sustainability initiatives clearly.
* Drive qualified leads through digital channels.

### Approach

1. Conducted an **audit** of existing digital assets.
2. Developed **interactive content**, including infographics and mini-videos.
3. Implemented a **social media campaign** targeting industry stakeholders.
4. Monitored **engagement metrics** and adjusted content weekly.

### Outcomes

* Achieved 35% increase in website traffic in 3 months.
* Secured 5 press mentions in top space and sustainability publications.
* Built a library of reusable multimedia assets for future campaigns.

### Lessons Learned

* Align messaging with sustainable development goals to attract eco-conscious clients.
* Early planning of multimedia content reduces bottlenecks.
* Data-driven content strategy enhances engagement.
